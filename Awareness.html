Community Awareness

Our Awareness Program focuses on educating communities about important social and health issues. Through workshops, campaigns, and school activities, we aim to spread knowledge and encourage positive change in everyday life.

Topics include hygiene, healthy habits, recycling, and community safety. We believe that awareness is the first step toward improvement and stronger, healthier communities.
