Monthly Food Drive

Our Monthly Food Drive helps provide nutritious meals to families in need. In October 2025, over 25 volunteers came together to distribute more than 300 food packages across the local community. The event focused on reducing hunger, preventing food waste, and spreading kindness through collective action.

The drive is part of our organization's ongoing effort to ensure that no family goes hungry. We are deeply thankful to all the donors and volunteers who made this event a success.
