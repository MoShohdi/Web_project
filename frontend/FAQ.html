Frequently Asked Questions

Here are some of the most common questions we receive about our organization and how to get involved.

1. How can I volunteer?

You can join our volunteer team by visiting the programs page and filling out the volunteer form.

2. Do I need any special skills to participate?

No, everyone is welcome! We provide simple training and guidance for all new volunteers.

3. Can I donate instead of volunteering?

Yes! We accept both material and financial donations to support our ongoing programs and community projects.

4. How do I stay updated on events?

You can check our News page regularly for updates and announcements about upcoming activities.
